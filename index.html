How to play
  • Each card you receive must be placed into one of your 12 empty slots — permanently.
  • Your chosen bot drafts the identical card sequence into its own hidden build.
  • Slot order is turn order: slot 1 fires first, slot 12 last.
  • Combat alternates — your slot, then theirs — until someone hits 0 HP or all slots resolve.
  • Burns tick at the start of a turn; shields soak the next hits; heals cap at 80.
